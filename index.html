About the Program

Brings PU and international schools together for cultural exchange, sustainability learning, and field immersion designed to build global collaboration and responsible tourism practices.

Cross-Cultural Understanding

Strengthen openness and respect through community-based learning, campus engagements, and cultural experiences.

Environmental Sustainability

Explore eco-sites and sustainability practices aligned with responsible travel and environmental protection.

Global Collaboration

Encourage international partnership-building through shared outputs, reflections, and presentations.

Community Immersion

Engage with local communities to appreciate heritage, livelihoods, and responsible tourism practices.

Responsible Travel Skills

Build practical habits in ethical travel, waste reduction, and sustainability-centered decision-making.
